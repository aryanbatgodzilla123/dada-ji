INFO ABOUT MY GRANDFATHER SHIV BIHARI SHARMA

Mr Shiv Bihari Sharma, the scion person of the Shiv Vilas group of directors brings with him a local Rajasthani flavour along with global exposure. A hotelier, an expert in gems and Jewellry, a connoisseur of textiles, Mr Shiv Bihari Sharma has traveled the globe, marking his presence across Italy, Spain, Greece and other European countries, taking the Sharma Groups Buisness interests and firmly establishing them across Europe. Multi linguistic, Mr Shiv Bihari Sharma speaks six international languages fluently, all the while retaining his grasp on all things Indian. Multi Faceted, Mr Shiv Bihari Sharma has not only established himself in his areas of expertise but now seeks to grow new ventures with the growing emerging sports market in India.
